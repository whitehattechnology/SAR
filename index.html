Our Mission

Established 2005

The Self-Awareness & Recovery (SAR)
program is designed to reduce recidivism
and incarceration rates. The goal is to help
adult and youth offenders heal from the
emotional wounds they experienced
during their formative years. Through self-
awareness we seek to explore the causative
factors that led to destructive life styles.
Through recovery we aim to teach healthy
coping skills for the preservation of
freedom and prosperity for those we serve.

Our Goals

Programs

➢ Emotional support workshops for ex-felons and their families.

➢ Relapse prevention plans.

➢ Youth intervention outreach programs for At-Risk Youth (juvenile facilities, schools, etc.)

Information Services

➢ Publish quarterly newsletters.

➢ SAR approved literature, curriculums,  DVD’s and materials (through correctional agencies across the country).

➢ Information on transitional housing, re- entry and employment opportunities.


Impact

➢ SAR community centers for people  who want to change their lives.

➢ Help to reduce the recidivism rates of  incarceration by working with parole and probation departments.
